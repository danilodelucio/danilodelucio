# 👤 About Me:
I'm Danilo, my passion for VFX is divided into three parts: Compositing, Matchmoving, and Pipeline.<br>
I am always studying and looking to improve my artistic and technical skills, making personal projects and creating/sharing some tools I develop.<br>

I love learning and teaching what I know, and I am always eager to grow in the VFX industry.
